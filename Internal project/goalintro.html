Vision Board Goal Setting

The ultimate tool for motivation and success in life using a combination of a vision board and a SMART goal.

Vision Board

A vision board is a collection of images which inspire you – could be parts of your dream life, or images which demonstrate a successful outcome from achieving your goals such as personal achievements. Having a visual representation is an effective way for motivation as it reminds you of your aspirations and knowing that everything you do affects your progress.



SMART Goal

A SMART goal stands for specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and timely.
When creating a SMART goal, these 5 factors need to be included.

Specific – your goal needs to focus around one specific area of your life and should be clear of what you want to achieve

Measurable – there should be a way to track your progress towards your goal so you can stay focused and
meet deadlines of smaller goals within the smart goal

Achievable – the goal should be possible for you to complete without factors out of
your control but should stretch your abilities

Relevant – is the goal you are creating effective and worth striving for? What are the benefits?

Timely – the goal should have a rough completion deadline to improve focus and motivation
